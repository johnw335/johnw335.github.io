Dealing with Conflict
September 24th, 2014

How do I deal with conflict?

This is the Compas of Shame, and it is supposed to show the range of ways people tend to deal with conflict and shame.

I have been looking at it for a while now and I cant seem to pinpoint where my reactions fall.

I can rule out the 'attack other' side of the compass, but I don't see and of the other options fitting perfectly either.

My reaction would probably display a little bit from each of the north, east, and south positions.

I would say that I tend to avoid conflict to the point of it being a fault, but when put in a situation where I am forced to deal with it I surprise myself.

As for shame, it's not really an issue for me. I am very comfortable with myself and the positions I hold. I believe that shame arises when there is a disconnect between beliefs we want to hold, and the actions we take.

I am sorry for the brevity of this post, but this topic is much more suited for an in debth conversation and not a post

If anyone is ever interested in continuing this conversation, I am always happy to have it!
