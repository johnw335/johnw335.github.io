Stereotype Threat
September 12th, 2014

Honestly, this is an odd topic for me to approach.

Being a white male in the southern United States, I kinda fall right in the middle of the majority. So, I don't think I have ever personally been effected by stereotype threat.

However, I can see this being a problem within the tech world.

Society has decided upon a pretty specific stereotype for memebers of the technology world. When people close their eyes and imagine a coder they probably all see someone who is...

* Young

* Male

* White
