"Week 3 Cultural Blog: Thinking Styles"
"August 23, 2014"

Let's take a minute to look at thinking styles. When I took the personal thinking style quiz I found that I think in a mix of AR (Abstract Random) and AS (Abstract Sequential).

Abstract thinkers tend to be strong visualizers and they rely on their imagination and intuition. The main difference between sequential and random thinkers is the way they organize information. Since my scores for each tied and they are complete opposites, i'm not exactly sure where I fall.

I think the abstract thinking style will fit well with DBC because of all of the group exercises. However, I will need to try to expand my concrete thinking style. This will really help me focus in a structured work environment. I am excited to get to DBC and find out how well I work with people of each thinking style.

It has been an interesting week working on JavaScript and I can't wait to put my new skills to work. Thanks!
