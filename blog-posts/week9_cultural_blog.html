How to ask questions
October 5th, 2014

Knowing how to ask questions is an important step in learning.

To begin, you should never feel ashamed about needing to ask a question.

(Especially with coding... this stuff is hard!)

The first step in asking a good question is to figure out exactly what you need help with.

It can be very difficult to put a general confused feeling into words. You should take your time and locate the exact thing causing confusion.

Next, you should phrase the question in a simple yet specific way.

If the question doesn't make complete sense to you, it is sure not going to make sense to anyone else.

Apart form that, the only important thing is to be open to the answer given

Ok so...

Find the source of your own confusion

Keep it simple

Be specific

and

Pay Attention

If you follow those simple rules, you will ask great questions.
